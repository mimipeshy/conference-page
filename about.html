ABOUT US

Praise Atmosphere is an annual family music experience that brings together gospel music Praise and worship artists from across Africa and is hosted by Multiple Award Winning Musician ~ Evelyn Wanjiru. Its Debut in 2018 brought together 12 Musicians from South Africa, Nigeria, Zimbabwe, Ghana, and Kenya. Artists included: Zaza, Mkhululi Bhebhe, Tembalami, Celestine Donkor, Emmy Kosgei, Mercy Masika, Evelyn Wanjiru, Pitson, Sarah K, Gloria Muliro, Size 8, Amani Aila and Praise Atmosphere Band.

Feel free to reach us for comments and enquiries about the worship experience
